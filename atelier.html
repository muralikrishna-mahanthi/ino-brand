CRAFTSMANSHIP AS ARCHITECTURE

Every garment at INO is conceived as a piece of functional architecture. From the internal canvas structures of our blazers to the weight of our custom-developed wools, we prioritize the engineering of silhouette above all.

MATERIAL PHILOSOPHY

We work exclusively with mills in Italy and Japan to source materials that respond to the human form. Our wools are selected for their 'architectural memory'—their ability to hold a shape while allowing fluid movement.
